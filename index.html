ABOUT

The Indonesian Street Festival is back for its second year in New York City, to showcase the beautiful cultural diversity that is Indonesia. This one day only event will feature an array of Indonesian dances, music, fashion, crafts, and food stalls that will give you a glimpse into the sights, sounds and tastes that the world’s largest archipelago has to offer. So save the date and come join us on August 27th 2016, in front of the Indonesian Consulate on5 East 68th Street, between 5th and Madison.
