ABOUT

Indonesian Street Festival is a celebration of Indonesian art, culture and gastronomy to commemorate Indonesia’s Independence Day on 17 August 2017. It is the third time ISF is held since 2015 that have tasted the wonders of Indonesia in New York City. ISF 2017’s theme “The Spice Islands” will take its attendees to cruise through Indonesia's archipelago and spices, including the widely-known one: nutmeg. This year, the festival will be held on 26 August 2017 at 1 pm along the bustling street of 68th between Madison and Fifth Avenue.
